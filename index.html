How It Works

Our End-to-End System. We Build It, Run It, and Optimise It.
You Just Take the Calls.

Step 01

Domain & Inbox Infrastructure

We build out a dedicated sending infrastructure, separate from your main domain, so your reputation is never at risk. Every inbox is warmed up correctly before a single email goes out.

Step 02

Lead Sourcing & Qualification

We don't just pull lists from Apollo or ZoomInfo and call it a day. We source and qualify against your ICP — industry, company size, revenue, job title, and any other criteria that matter to you. Then we validate every email before it goes into a sequence.

Step 03

Copywriting & Sequencing

We write multiple messaging angles across different segments of your market. Seniority, role, company stage — each gets copy built around what actually matters to them. We layer in real signals where we can: recent hires, funding rounds, news.

Step 04

Reply Handling & Booking

When a prospect replies, we handle it. We manage objections, warm them up, and get a time locked in on your calendar. You show up to a meeting that's already been qualified. That's it.

Step 05

Continuous Optimisation

We track what's working, cut what isn't, and double down on the angles that are driving replies and bookings. This is where most agencies leave 30–50% of potential meetings on the table. We don't.

There are three ways to work with a cold email agency.

Option 01

Pay a retainer

$2,000–$5,000 a month, whether you close clients or not. You carry all the risk. The agency gets paid regardless.

Option 02

A "performance" deal written in the agency's favour

Sounds fair until you read the fine print. They get paid per reply or per "interested response" — a definition loose enough to include people who were never going to buy.

Option 03

Work with us

You pay per qualified call booked. That's it. Every prospect is routed through your qualification filter before they can book — wrong industry, wrong title, wrong budget, they don't get through. You only ever pay for calls that match your ICP.

There is a small one-time setup fee to cover infrastructure. After that, you pay for results and nothing else.

Here's Exactly What Happens After You Sign On

Week 1

Onboarding

We set up your domains and inboxes, and get up to speed on your offer, your ICP, and the qualification criteria for calls. We also spend time reviewing as many of your recent sales calls as possible, ideally across the last three months.
